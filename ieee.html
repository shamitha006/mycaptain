Institute for Electrical and Electronics Engineers is an entity aimed at inculcating the sprirt of wisdom and knowledge. BMSCE IEEE Student Branch was founded in 2009 under the guidance of our first branch counselor and former vice principal, Dr. Ravishankar Deekshit. The founding chair for the student branch was Ashwith Jerome Rego of the EEE department. With events and workshops being conducted throughout the year, we make sure our members are kept up to date with the evolving needs of our technological world. In the initial years, the student branch mainly focused on workshops and technical talks.
